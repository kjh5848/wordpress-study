This plugin displays a floating KakaoTalk OpenChat button on your website, allowing visitors to easily contact you through KakaoTalk OpenChat.

== Description ==

The "KakaoTalk OpenChat Floating Call-to-Action Button" plugin adds a convenient floating button to your website that links directly to your KakaoTalk OpenChat. This plugin makes it easier for your visitors to engage in conversation with you or your team, thereby enhancing user interaction and experience.

The icon for the floating button and plugin banner was designed by Illustrator 푸르릉(Purreung). Visit her website at [https://purreung.com](https://purreung.com) for more amazing designs.

Key Features:
- Easy to set up and customize.
- Floating button that stays visible on all pages.
- Customizable KakaoTalk OpenChat URL.
- Subtle drop shadow for better visibility without being intrusive.
- Admin settings to easily manage the KakaoTalk OpenChat URL.

For more information and updates, visit my website at [https://mytory.net](https://mytory.net).

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/kakaotalk-openchat-button` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Use the Settings > 카카오 오픈챗 screen to configure the plugin.
4. Enter your KakaoTalk OpenChat URL in the provided field.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I find my KakaoTalk OpenChat URL? =

Your KakaoTalk OpenChat URL is the link to your KakaoTalk OpenChat room. You can find it in your KakaoTalk OpenChat settings.
